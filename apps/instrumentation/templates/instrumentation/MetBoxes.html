- MetBoxes -

A series of 20 Metboxes is being acquired to get meteorological data in a desired location. These metboxes will be mobiles and self-sufficient with solar panel and batteries.

Each of them will be equipped with a Climavue 50 sensor (https://www.campbellsci.ca/climavue-50) , an additional thermometer, a datalogger and many optional equipment will be available to add on the tripod. The Climavue 50 sensor is acting as a pyranometer, an anemometer, a thermometer, a relative humidity sensor, a Drip Counter Rain Gage.
