- Mobile Urban Weather Station (MUST) -

Picture taken from the Sajess website

The MUST station is a mobile lab providing a platform for in-situ meteorological and atmospheric sciences studies. The trailer allows the field team to move the instruments where the meteorological phenomena studied have chances to happen.

Instruments included:

  • Micro Rain Radar (MRR Pro)
  • OTT Parsivel laser disdrometer
  • Instruments to measure meteorological variables near the soil surface as temperature, humidity, wind, net radiation, soil temperature, precipitations.
  • A System to study flux (Un système de covariance de tourbillon pour calculer les flux de surfaces de masse et d'énergie)
