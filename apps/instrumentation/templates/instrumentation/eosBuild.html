- Earth Observation System-Laboratory building-

Photo credit: Eve Bigras

 

This new building opened in 2022 and is dedicated to Biology and meteorological research as well as environmental isotopic measurements.

Available inside are a wet laboratory for biology and isotopic measurements and an office base for meteorological field deployment.

The biology laboratory is equipped with:

  • A FX9 Biochamber
  • Orbital shaker Eppendorf 2050
  • Shaker Oven Eppendorf 40R
  • Agilent Bioanalyzer
  • Tecan plate reader with monochromator
  • Undercounter Fridge Panasonic TSU4RWN6
  • Fridge Panasonic MPR722PA
  • Freezer Panasonic MDFMU539HLPA
  • Spectrophotometer Agilent Cary 60 with 18 cells holder and 100mm path cells
  • 3 thermocyclers Biorad T100
  • Vortex mixers
  • Mini Corning centrifuges
  • Water purification system Smart2pure6
  • Autoclave Tuttnauer 3870ELP
  • Sartorius Tacta Pipettes
  • Biological Safety Cabinet Baker Sterilguard III SG304
  • Chemical hood
  • DNA extraction system Autogen Quickgene 810
  • Short gene sequencer (to come)
  • Drying oven Binder ED115 UL
  • pH meter Mettler Toledo FiveEasy Plus FP20
  • 1 Axiovert microscopes
  • 2D imaging system (to come)
