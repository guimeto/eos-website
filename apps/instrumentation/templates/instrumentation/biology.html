- Molecular biology laboratory -

Located downtown Montreal, this laboratory is having 2 main areas, one dedicated to eDNA work (molecular biology lab) and the other one where the dirty specimens are arriving. The molecular biology laboratory is designed with the following characteristics:

  • Positive pressure from the cleanest room to the “dirtiest” room
  • An anteroom for dressing
  • A clean room
  • An eDNA room
