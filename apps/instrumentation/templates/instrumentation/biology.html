- Molecular biology laboratory -

Located downtown Montreal, this laboratory is having 2 main areas, one dedicated to eDNA work (molecular biology lab) and the other one where the dirty specimens are arriving. The molecular biology laboratory is designed with the following characteristics:

  • Positive pressure from the cleanest room to the “dirtiest” room
  • An anteroom for dressing
  • A clean room
  • An eDNA room

The molecular biology laboratory is equipped with:

  • ddPCR instrument-QIAcuity one 5 plex
  • Agilent Bioanalyzer
  • Tecan Spark Plate reader
  • Pipettes Sartorius Tacta
  • Mini Corning centrifuges
  • pH meter
  • Vortex mixers
  • Barnstead Micropure Water purification system
  • Autogen Quickgene 810
  • Chemical hood
  • AirClean hood 48 inches
  • Biological safety cabinet Baker Sterilguard III SG304
  • Panasonic sliding door Fridge MPR1014PA
  • Undercounter Freezer Panasonic SFL6111WPA
  • Freezer Panasonic MDFU5312PA
  • Steris autoclave Amsco 250LS
  • Steris dishwasher Reliance 400XLS
  • Orbital shaker Eppendorf 2050
  • Digital incubator Boekel
  • Fluorometer Qbit
  • Incubator Fisherbrand
  • Flammable cabinets-A full room of cabinet is available for sample storage
  • 3 Eppendorf thermocyclers (1 Mastercycler and 2 Eco)
