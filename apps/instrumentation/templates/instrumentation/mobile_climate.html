- Mobile climate sentinels -

Among the portable meteorological instruments, 4 mobile tripods will be available.

 

Met tripod

The Met tripod is designed to obtain most of the measurements made by a regular climate sentinel with the same data quality.

It will be equipped with:

  • Datalogger CR310 WIFI
  • GPS
  • Ultrasonic WindSensor LUFFTV200A
  • Infrared radiometer SI-111-SS
  • NR01-4 component Net Radiometer
  • Hotplate? Changer pour Climavue?
  • Hygrovue10-Digital temperature and relative humidity
  • Water content reflectometer CS655-L
  • Barovue10-digital barometric pressure sensor
  • SR50A-sonic ranger
  • 10ft stainless steel tripod
  • 20ft stainless steel tripod

 

Flux tripod

  • Datalogger CR310 WIFI
  • GPS (GPS16X-HVS)
  • IRGASON
  • Soil heat flux plates
  • Averaging Soil temperature probes
  • Water content reflectometer
  • Hygrovue10-Digital temperature and relative humidity
  • NR01-4 component Net Radiometer
  • 10ft stainless steel tripod

 

Agro/Bio tripod

  • Datalogger CR310 WIFI
  • GPS
  • Ultrasonic WindSensor LUFFTV200A
  • Kipp & Zonen PAR quantum sensor
  • Hygrovue10 Temperature and relative humidity
  • SoilVUE10-Soil moisture profile sensor
  • Temperature probe 109
  • Leaf wetness sensor
  • 10ft stainless steel tripod CM110 and guy wires

 

Visibility tripod

  • Datalogger CR310 WIFI
  • 10ft stainless steel tripod
  • OTT Parsivel2 sensor
