Met tripod

The Met tripod is designed to obtain most of the measurements made by a regular climate sentinel with the same data quality.

-Infrared radiometer SI-111-SS: https://s.campbellsci.com/documents/us/manuals/si-111.pdf

-NR01-4 component Net Radiometer: https://www.campbellsci.ca/nr01
