- Historic -

EOS-Adaptable Earth Observation System is a CFI and Quebec funded project involving Biology and Atmospheric research professors from McGill University and UQAM. Scientific partners are UdeS, ULaval, UQTR.

  • January 2020

    - 15-minutes data are now available from our Gault reserve Weather Station. The picture is showing temperature data from last night until now (UTC time). Available data: flux, temperature at 8 levels, soil temperature, soil water content, humidity, atmospheric pressure, solar radiation, Water content, Visibility, Present weather, Wind speed, wind direction, snow depth, cloud altitude etc.

  • March 2019

    The assembly of our first Weather Station is almost completed. This station is located at the Gault natural reserve in Saint-Hilaire.
